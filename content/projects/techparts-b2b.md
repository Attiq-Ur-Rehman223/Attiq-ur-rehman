## Project Overview

TechParts needed a B2B eCommerce solution that could replace their legacy order-by-phone system. The platform required complex tiered pricing, credit account management, and real-time inventory sync from their SAP ERP system.

## Key Features

- **Tiered wholesale pricing** based on customer account level (5 tiers)
- **Custom quote request system** for bulk and special orders
- **Net-30/60 invoicing** with automated accounts receivable
- **SAP ERP integration** via custom REST API connector for inventory and order sync
- **Restricted catalog** — different product visibility per customer account type
- **CSV bulk ordering** for procurement teams

## Results

- $1.2M revenue in first 12 months (40% above projections)
- 78% reduction in manual order processing time
- 320 active B2B accounts onboarded in year one
- 99.9% uptime with zero critical failures
